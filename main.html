Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known for its art, culture, and world-famous cuisine. It's a dream destination for travelers worldwide.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – the most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – home to the Mona Lisa and thousands of artworks.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – a masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – a lively avenue filled with shops and cafés.
  5. Seine River Cruise – offers stunning views of the city.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – buttery, flaky pastries.
  • Crêpes – thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • Macarons – colorful, delicate French cookies.
  • French Onion Soup – rich, cheesy comfort food.
  • Ratatouille – a traditional vegetable stew.
